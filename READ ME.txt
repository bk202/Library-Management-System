MySQL workbench is required for execution of program.
MySQL workbench can be downloaded and installed at https://dev.mysql.com/downloads/file/?id=470090.

To execute the program:
Open the execution program located at: ../Library-Management-System/bin/Debug/LibraryManagementSystem.exe

To use the program:
Login with your root account of local MySQL server.
